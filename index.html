About

I am very passionate about Video Games. That passion for video games comes from my childhood. I spent a large amount of my childhood playing PlayStation 2 and Xbox 360 games like Burnout: Revenge (The only reason I even played Burnout was because it was on my Need For Speed: Most Wanted Demo list) and Assassins Creed. I was really fascinated by the experiences I had and the worlds I jumped between. From there I decided to put some of my effort into writing those stories and building those world. Now, I am working on my plan to open a Video Game Development studio in Portland, Oregon.

Voila! Movie Trivia

Voila! Movie Trivia was a project I worked on while participating in the TECHCHARLOTTE Internship Program. It was designed as movie trivia web app targeted at movie lovers, children, and even tv fanatics.

ACE 2015, 2016, 2017

Coda Court and the Obama Presidential Libraries were projects I worked on during my time in the ACE Mentorship Program. I worked on the building architecture, 3D Model, and 2D design for both projects. For Coda Court, I conducted extensive research on the neighborhood our project would effect as well as looking for ways to include Black history into the projet. For the Obama Presidential Library, I worked on a cost map.

  • Program: ACE Mentorship Program
  • Date: 2015, 2016, 2017
  • Service: 3D Modeling, Architect
